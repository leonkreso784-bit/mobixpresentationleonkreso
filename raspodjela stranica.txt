MOBIX – AI-Powered Travel Platform
Presentation Structure & Slide Content
Slide 1 – What is MOBIX?

Title: What is MOBIX?

Content:

MOBIX is an AI-powered digital travel platform

It combines:

Travel planning

Artificial intelligence

Social discovery

Marketplace logic

MOBIX transforms travel planning from a fragmented process into a single intelligent flow

Key message:
MOBIX is not a booking site — it is a travel operating system.

Slide 2 – The Problem MOBIX Solves

Title: The Problem with Travel Planning Today

Content:

Travel planning is fragmented across many platforms

Users must manually compare flights, hotels, attractions, transport

Prices change constantly and are difficult to optimize

Travelers often miss experiences they did not know existed

Planning is time-consuming, stressful, and inefficient

Key message:
Travel planning today requires too many tools and too many decisions.

Slide 3 – The MOBIX Solution

Title: MOBIX as the Solution

Content:

MOBIX centralizes the entire travel process

Users interact with one AI assistant instead of multiple platforms

The AI:

Understands user preferences

Explains recommendations

Builds complete travel plans

Everything happens inside one ecosystem

Key message:
MOBIX reduces complexity by replacing search with intelligence.

Slide 4 – How MOBIX Works (High Level)

Title: How MOBIX Works

Content (flow):

User creates a profile and sets preferences

AI analyzes budget, interests, and travel style

User interacts with chatbot or discovery feed

MOBIX generates:

Transport options

Accommodation

Attractions and restaurants

User saves content into Travel Notes

Travel Notes become a complete, bookable itinerary

Key message:
Intent → AI → Structured travel plan.

Slide 5 – AI Chatbot Logic

Title: AI Chatbot – Three Core Modes

Content:

Recommendation Mode

Suggests destinations, hotels, restaurants, attractions

Based on user profile and behavior

Conversational Mode

Natural conversation (who are you, how does this work, etc.)

Trip Planning Mode

Full itineraries with cards and explanations

Transport, stay, activities, return options

Key message:
The chatbot adapts its behavior to user intent.

Users build trips visually and intuitively.
